The 2015 World Series

New York Mets and Kansas City Royals

The 2015 World Series was a very exciting appearance for Mets fans. The Mets had been in quite the dry spell for the last several years, and they seemingly came out of nowhere in 2015. Led by a team of younger players, the Mets swept the powerhouse Cubs in the National League Championship Series, whereas the Royals seemed to have much more trouble in their League Championship Series. The Mets seemed to have all of the momentum, yet they found themselves down 2 games to none early on. However, they convincingly won game 3 and looked poised to bounce back in the latter haf of the series. However, they were truely outmatched as the Royals had seemingly no problem with them at all in games 4 and 5. This was another disappointing series for the Mets, as they looked like the best team in the postseason leading up to the World Series, but got outplayed in every aspect.
